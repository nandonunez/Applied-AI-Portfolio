This project delivers an interactive dashboard for monitoring, analyzing, and visualizing waste flows and energy recovery at a large urban treatment facility. The solution integrates multiple public datasets and provides actionable insights for operational and strategic decision-making.

### **Workflow**
1. **Data Integration:** Combines datasets on waste inputs, material recovery, energy, and biogas generation.
2. **Visualization:** Interactive dashboards with annual, monthly, and plant-level trends, color-coded by waste fraction and output type.
3. **Geospatial Analytics:** Maps treatment plants and enables drill-down analytics by location.
4. **User Interaction:** Filters by year, waste fraction, service type, district, plant, and time range.
